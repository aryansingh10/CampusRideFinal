Our Services

Ride Hailing

The core service allows users to request a ride from their current location to a desired destination. This includes options like standard cars, SUVs, and sometimes even luxury or specialty vehicles.

Carpooling

This service enables users heading in the same direction to share a ride and split the fare. It's an eco-friendly option that helps reduce traffic congestion.

Bike and Scooter Rentals

In addition to cars, ride-sharing apps sometimes offer options for renting bicycles, scooters, or other forms of micro-mobility.
